About Us

We are an online Christmas shop located in Sligo, that sells all kinds of Christmas related products from Christmas posters to Christmas trees. Here you will be able to find and purchase Christmas products at affordable prices with free delivery. We quality test all our products before being dispatched for delivery to ensure you get the best possible product.

You can purchase a Christmas product here by registering an account with us and then logging in. We only accept card payment and you are entitled to return the product if you are unsatisfied with it and get a full refund for up to 7 days after it has been dispatched. We hope you enjoy what we have to offer Merry Christmas!
